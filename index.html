Micro Budgeting Privacy Policy

100% offline, privacy-first personal finance and micro-budgeting app with automatic SMS expense parsing.

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Overview

Micro Budgeting (“the App”) is a mobile application published by Michael (com.michael.microbudgeting). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how that information is used, and your choices.

Privacy principle: The App is designed to operate locally on your device. The App does not request network access, does not use third-party analytics, and does not transmit your personal files or data to external servers.

Information We Access

To provide its features, the App may access information on your device that you grant permission for, such as files, media, app usage data, or other data types required by the features you use. Processing occurs on your device unless the App explicitly states otherwise in its store listing or in-app disclosures.

Information Stored on Your Device

The App may store preferences, caches, logs, or other operational data locally on your device to improve performance and provide history or settings. This data remains on your device unless you clear app data or uninstall the App.

Permissions

Depending on your device and Android version, the App may request permissions such as:

  • READ SMS

You can deny or revoke permissions in your device settings. Some features may be limited without the relevant permission.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not use third-party advertising analytics unless disclosed in the app listing
  • We do not collect more data than needed for the App’s stated features

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
